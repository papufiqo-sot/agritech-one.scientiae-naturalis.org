About AgriTech-One 2026

This European Scientific Conference offers 5 Continuing Education Units (CEUs) or Continuing Professional Development credits (CDPs) for agronomists. AgriTech-One is a Strategic Partner of EU Climate Pact and also a FAO-COP 31 Side event. The conference will bring together researchers, risk assessors, risk managers and stakeholders to share the latest scientific and practical advances and support evidence-based risk management and policy decisions. AgriTech-One 2026 is Digital Poster& Asynchronous Video based Scientific Conference. AgriTech-One 2026 focus: AI-driven biotech in agrifood systems.
